## ğŸ“§ Setting Up the Contact Form

The contact form uses **FormSubmit.co** (a free, secure email forwarder).

1.  **Open the website** in your browser.
2.  **Scroll to the "Contact" section**.
3.  **Fill out the form** with your email and a test message.
4.  **Click "Send Message"**.
5.  You will be redirected to a FormSubmit page.
6.  **IMPORTANT**: Go to your email inbox (`vivekjain.vj.2019@gmail.com`). You will see an email from FormSubmit trying to "Activate" this form. **Click the Activation Link**.

Once activated, all future messages will arrive directly in your inbox without the activation screen.
